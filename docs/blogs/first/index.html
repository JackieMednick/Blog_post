Hou Yi Hot Pot

92 Hester St, New York, NY 10002

Hou Yi Hot Pot is one of my favorite hot pot places to go to. It is all you can eat with free ice cream if the food gets too spicy. This also was the first place I ever ate hot pot at and to this day, I’ve never had better.

9/10
