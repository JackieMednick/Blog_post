Raku

57 W 76th St, New York, NY 10023

This is my favorite sushi place I have ever tried, even if you order it online for delivery. I am particular about my sushi and hate fish bones, but I have never found a fish bone in any of my meals from here. The sushi melts in your mouth, and you easily become addicted to it.

10/10
